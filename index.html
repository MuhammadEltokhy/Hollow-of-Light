Game Details

Hollow Knight is a 2D Metroidvania action-adventure game, that takes place in Hallownest, a fictional ancient kingdom. The player controls an insectoid, silent, nameless knight while exploring the underground world.

The game emphasizes skill-based gameplay with Dark Souls-inspired mechanics, including the ability to recover lost currency and health by defeating enemies or finding Soul totems.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Is Hollow Knight a difficult game?

Yes, Hollow Knight is known for its challenging gameplay. Players must master precise platforming, timing, and combat skills. However, with practice and exploration, the game becomes deeply rewarding.

How long does it take to complete Hollow Knight?

A standard playthrough takes around 25-30 hours. Completionists aiming for 100% can expect to spend over 50 hours exploring every secret and conquering every boss.

What platforms is Hollow Knight available on?

Hollow Knight is available on PC, macOS, Nintendo Switch, PlayStation 4, and Xbox One. It runs smoothly across all platforms with gamepad and keyboard support.
