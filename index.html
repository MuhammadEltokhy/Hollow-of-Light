Game Details

Hollow Knight is a 2D Metroidvania action-adventure game, that takes place in Hallownest, a fictional ancient kingdom. The player controls an insectoid, silent, nameless knight while exploring the underground world.

The game emphasizes skill-based gameplay with Dark Souls-inspired mechanics, including the ability to recover lost currency and health by defeating enemies or finding Soul totems.
